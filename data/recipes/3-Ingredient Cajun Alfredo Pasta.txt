# 3-Ingredient Cajun Alfredo Pasta

PREP TIME: 3 mins  
COOK TIME: 12 mins  
TOTAL TIME: 15 mins  
SERVINGS: 4 to 6 servings  

## Ingredients

- 8 ounces penne pasta
- 1 tablespoon olive or vegetable oil
- 13 ounces Cajun andouille smoked sausage
- 1 (15-ounce) jar store-bought alfredo sauce
- 2 tablespoons water

## Method

1. Cook the pasta:  
   Bring a large pot of water to a boil over high heat. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. While the water is coming to a boil and the pasta is cooking, move on to the remaining steps. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

2. Slice and sear the sausage:  
   Slice the sausage into roughly quarter-inch rounds. Add oil to a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add sausage and cook until browned on both sides, about 2 minutes per side. Reduce heat to medium-low.

3. Make the sauce:  
   Pour the jar of Alfredo sauce into the skillet with the sausage. Add water to the empty jar, shake to loosen remaining sauce, then pour into the skillet. Stir constantly and cook until warmed through, about 2 minutes.

4. Add pasta:  
   Add cooked pasta to the skillet and stir to combine. Remove from heat and serve immediately.

   Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Do not freeze, as the sauce texture will change.
