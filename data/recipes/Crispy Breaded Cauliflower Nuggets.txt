# Crispy Breaded Cauliflower “Nuggets”

PREP TIME: 40 mins  
COOK TIME: 30 mins  
TOTAL TIME: 70 mins  
SERVINGS: 4 to 6 servings  

## Ingredients

- Nonstick cooking spray
- 3/4 cup unflavored plant-based milk (oat, cashew, and soy work best) or regular milk
- 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons Dijon or brown mustard
- 3/4 teaspoon fine salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast (or finely grated parmesan)
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 medium head cauliflower (about 2 pounds), cut into bite-sized florets
- Marinara sauce, for serving

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F:  
   Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, spray with cooking spray, and set aside.

2. Make the batter and breading mixture:  
   In a large bowl, combine the milk, flour, mustard, salt, and pepper. Whisk until a smooth batter forms. Set aside.  
   In a medium bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, nutritional yeast, garlic, and oregano. Toss to incorporate. Set aside.

3. Dredge the cauliflower:  
   Add the cauliflower to the bowl with the batter. Using a rubber spatula, toss until all the florets are evenly coated. Then, working with one floret at a time, add the cauliflower to the breadcrumbs and gently toss to coat. Place on the prepared baking sheet and continue with the remaining cauliflower. Be sure to arrange the cauliflower in a single layer.

4. Bake the cauliflower:  
   Spray the cauliflower generously with cooking spray, and bake until crispy and golden brown, 30 to 35 minutes.

5. Serve:  
   Serve immediately alongside warm marinara.  
   Refrigerate leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days. For best results, reheat in the oven to revive its crispness.
