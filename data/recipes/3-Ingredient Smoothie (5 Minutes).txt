# 3-Ingredient Smoothie (5 Minutes)

PREP TIME: 3 mins
COOK TIME: 5 mins
TOTAL TIME: 8 mins
SERVINGS: 1 serving
YIELD: 1 smoothie

## Ingredients

- 1 cup vanilla kefir
- 1 medium ripe banana, cut into 1-inch pieces and frozen
- 1 cup frozen mixed berries

## Method

1. Add the ingredients to the blender: In this order, add kefir, then banana, then berries. Start on low speed for several seconds, then very slowly increase the speed until a smooth and creamy mixture forms. Stir once or twice if needed. If your blender can’t quite do it, add water or milk, a small splash at a time, until the mixture comes together.
